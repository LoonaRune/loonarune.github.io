  • Starting Gravity: 1.079 + fruit
  • Ending Gravity:
  • Alcohol Percentage:
  • Yeast: Lalvin EC-1118

Ingredients

  • 11 lbs honey (Mike's summer wildflower 2019)
  • 8 lbs kiwi (fresh, grocery store)
  • 4 lbs strawberry (fresh, grocery store)
  • 4 lbs apples (fresh, grocery store)
  • 6 oz blackberries (fresh, grocery store)

Process

  1. Froze all fruit
  2. Partially thawed and quartered apples and kiwis
  3. Removed stems from strawberries
  4. Dumped fruit into vessel as it was being prepared
  5. Added honey.
  6. Topped up with water
  7. Stir as much as possible (this will be hard)
  8. Pitch

Log

June 10 2023

Racked off fruit

Notes and Observations

  • Should have used pectic enzyme to break down the fruit better
  • Should have cut the fruit before freezing
  • Should have thawed the fruit more thoroughly before putting in the vessel. Ended up waiting a day for the temp to rise enough to be comfortable pitching the yeast.
  • Would probably have been helpful to weigh the fruit down in a bag rather than letting it float freely
