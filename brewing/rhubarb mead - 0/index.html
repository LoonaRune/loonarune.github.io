Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs honey (Mike's summer 2019)
  • 1 lb rhubarb (Lauren's garden)

Log

  1. Chop rhubarb and bag.
  2. Freeze rhubarb for a day.
  3. Simmer rhubarb in water for extraction
  4. Discard rhubarb chunks which should be devoid of color at this point
  5. Mix the rhubarb extract with the honey and top off vessel with water
  6. Stir
  7. Pitch
